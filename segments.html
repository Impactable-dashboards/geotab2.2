The strategic read
◆ Where the expansion sits

Operations is who you reach. Safety is who you need.

The operational personas carry the program's reach today, which is the right base. The growth is in Safety, Risk, and Compliance, the buying center that owns cameras and driver behavior and the exact committee Samsara targets to get inside an account. The camera-expansion program is built to open that committee inside existing fleet customers before a competitor frames the conversation. Multithreading from a known operations contact into the safety owner is the play.
