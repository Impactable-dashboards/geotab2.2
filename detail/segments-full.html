Key findings

Opportunity

The engine is aimed at the right buyer.

Operations is the #1 function at 34% of reach, enterprise companies (1,001+) take 55%, and the top job titles are Operations, Logistics, Supply Chain, Transportation, and Fleet Managers. Fleet Manager shows the single best title CTR at 0.45%. Core targeting is sound — the program reaches fleet decision-makers, not noise.

Critical

One in five impressions reaches an off-ICP function.

20% of reach goes to Human Resources, Education, Community & Social Services, Healthcare, Legal, Arts, and Media — functions with no role in a fleet-telematics purchase. That is roughly $75,349 of implied YTD reach value redirectable to ICP audiences. Tightening function targeting is the single clearest efficiency move.

Critical

APAC runs at a third of the efficiency of every other region.

APAC CTR is 0.12% against 0.31–0.46% elsewhere, at the highest CPC ($8.64). The drag is Indonesia and the Philippines: ~1.5M impressions at ~0.07% CTR. APAC is the clearest region-level restructure or pause-and-rebuild candidate.

Watch

"Senior" is the biggest bucket but it is not a decision-maker tier.

LinkedIn "Senior" (senior individual contributors) is 43% of all reach — more than Manager, Director, VP and CXO combined are clustered below it. Confirmed decision-maker tiers (Manager→CXO+Owner) sit around ~48%. Worth validating that "Senior" reach maps to fleet influencers, not adjacent ICs.

Watch

EMEA looks efficient but its conversions don't show up.

EMEA has the best CTR (0.46%) and lowest CPC ($4.94) on $138,774 of spend, yet only 6 tracked site conversions vs 5 form leads. This is a measurement gap, not necessarily a performance gap — EMEA likely runs on lead forms while the Insight Tag underfires. Fix before the EBR so EMEA isn't misread as underperforming.

Critical

Entry-level engages hard — and that's a targeting flag.

Entry-level is 9% of reach with the highest engagement rate (1.36%) of any seniority. High engagement from a non-buying tier inflates surface metrics without advancing pipeline. Exclude or down-weight Entry/Training to concentrate budget on buyers.
