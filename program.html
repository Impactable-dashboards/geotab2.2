● The efficiency read

Mid-funnel does the most on the least, and BOF is climbing

The middle of the funnel carries roughly a third of the spend yet drives the program's most efficient qualified action, and over the recent 90 days the bottom of the funnel has climbed to 10% program-wide. That is the rebalance case proving itself: weight is already shifting from awareness toward the layers that convert. The exact cost-per-action comparison firms up as conversion definitions are normalized across channels, and the direction is already clear.

● Recommendation · regional budget

Trim APAC, concentrate where it converts

APAC is carrying about $20K over the trailing 90 days for limited engagement. We recommend bringing APAC down to a $10K maintenance level and moving the balance into the mid and bottom-funnel layers in North America, where the same dollars are converting. LATAM holds at its current level; at comparable spend it is reaching efficiently.
