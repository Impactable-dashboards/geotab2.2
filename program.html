● The efficiency read

Mid-funnel does the most on the least, and BOF is climbing

The middle of the funnel carries roughly a third of the spend yet drives the program's most efficient qualified action, and over the recent 90 days the bottom of the funnel has climbed to 10% program-wide. That is the rebalance case proving itself: weight is already shifting from awareness toward the layers that convert. The exact cost-per-action comparison stays held until conversion definitions are normalized across channels, so we lead with the direction, not a fragile number.
