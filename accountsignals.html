● Signal divergence · the orchestration case

Rogers Communications

Rogers was a top LinkedIn engager in April, then went to zero in May. In the same window 6sense shows it as the single biggest surge, 232 web visits. That is real, high intent we are generating and not catching on LinkedIn. It is the clearest argument in the program for a persistent, organization-level retargeting layer that holds the signal instead of letting it fall through. Worth confirming the account was not simply moved to another campaign.

◆ Activation gaps · top plays to assign

Verizon & Allied Universal

Verizon engages across all five personas and is still unowned, the widest reach with no rep. Allied Universal engages across all five, is rising, carries a seven-week 6sense surge, and has never had a 1:1 activated, the widest intent-to-action gap in the book. Both are Top 25 escalations: assign an owner, stand up the play.
